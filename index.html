frequently asked questions

What are the most common applications for Avio-Gyro?

The great flexibility of Avio-Gyro makes it suitable for very different applications. In fact, in addition to sports can operate on sectors such as Defense, Civil Protection, agricultural work or can be scaled to use it in drone version.

What is the full load capacity of Avio-Gyro?

The construction of Avio-Gyro allows it to have an empty weight of just over 200kg. This implies, considering the maximum load capacity in force of 600kg, a full load capacity of about 380kg.
